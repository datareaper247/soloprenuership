# Extended Role Library

These roles are real and useful — just not the ones a solo founder reaches for daily.

They come into play at:
- $50K+ MRR (dedicated HR, Legal, RevOps)
- Team of 3+ (DevOps, QA, dedicated social/email)
- Enterprise motion (Sales Engineer, Revenue Operations)
- International expansion (Localization, International Market Manager)
- Specific campaigns (Video Producer, Brand Designer, PR)
