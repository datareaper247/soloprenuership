# Vision: The Solo Billion-Dollar Company

## The Thesis

We are entering an era where a single focused human, with the right AI framework, can build what previously required 50-500 people.

This is not a prediction. It's already happening:

- **Midjourney** — peaked at $200M ARR with 11 employees
- **Perplexity** — $500M+ ARR trajectory, small team
- **Notion AI**, **Superhuman**, **Linear** — high revenue per employee
- **Solo developers** shipping products used by millions

The bottleneck is no longer headcount. The bottleneck is **leverage** — knowing how to orchestrate AI agents across every business function simultaneously.

## Why Now?

**5 forces converging in 2025-2026**:

1. **LLM capability** — Claude, GPT-4, Gemini can now perform knowledge work at professional level
2. **Agent frameworks** — Claude Code, AutoGPT, LangChain enable autonomous task execution
3. **API ecosystem** — Every tool has an API. Automation is composable.
4. **AI-native tooling** — Cursor, Claude Code, Copilot reduce engineering time 5-10x
5. **Infrastructure commoditization** — Supabase, Vercel, Railway eliminated ops overhead

The window for the solo billion-dollar company is **open right now**.

It won't stay open forever. Larger companies will adapt. Competition will increase. But for the next 3-5 years, a solo founder with the right framework can outcompete teams.
