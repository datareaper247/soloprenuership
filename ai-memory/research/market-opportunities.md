## Exit Market Analysis (2024-2026)

### Current Multiples
| Type | Multiple |
|---|---|
| Micro-SaaS (<$1M ARR) | 5-7x revenue |
| Profitable micro-SaaS | 4-6x revenue |
| Vertical SaaS | 7-9.5x revenue (25-30% premium) |
| Unprofitable SaaS | 1-2x (acquihire only) |

### Highest-Multiple Niches
1. Cemetery/Funeral homes — $5B TAM, zero VC competition, 6-8x
2. Contractor dispatch (HVAC, plumbing) — $20B TAM, PE consolidators active
3. Dental practice management — $10B TAM, compliance-sticky
4. Legal practice software — $10B TAM, compliance-driven
5. Therapy/counseling tools — $3B TAM, growing

### What Acquirers Care About (Priority Order)
1. Profitability (EBITDA 20%+)
2. Retention (90%+ annual)
3. CAC efficiency (<12mo payback)
4. Gross margins (70%+)
5. Growth (20%+ YoY)

**Key insight**: Profitability > Growth. Boring > Flashy. Vertical > Horizontal.

### 3-Year Exit Roadmap
- Year 1: PMF, $1K MRR
- Year 2: Profitability, $25K MRR, 90%+ retention
- Year 3: $50K MRR, position for exit
- Result: $600K ARR × 5-6x = **$3-3.6M exit**
