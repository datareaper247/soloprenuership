# Pharmacy PBM Audit Software

## The Pain
- Average PBM audit clawback: $26,144 per independent pharmacy
- PAAS members save 93% of that ($24,314 average)
- Audits are more aggressive than ever in 2025

## Market
- 19K-23K independent pharmacies in US
- $172.56B independent pharmacy market (4.26% CAGR)
- TAM if 30% adopt: $149M
- Realistic Year 1 SOM: $300-600K

## Regulatory Tailwind
- Feb 2026: Congress passed landmark PBM reforms (2026 CAA)
- 100% rebate pass-through required (2028-2029)
- FTC lawsuit against top 3 PBMs
- Bipartisan support = durable tailwind

## Competitive Landscape
1. PAAS National (30+ years, membership model) — not SaaS
2. Pharmacy Audit Pro (AI SaaS, emerging)
3. PillarRx (third-party services)
4. InsourceRx (automation)
- No VC-backed SaaS winner yet — gap exists

## Why Score Is 6.5 (Not Higher)
- Services preference (pharmacies trust people over software)
- Regulatory complexity (HIPAA, PBM contracts)
- Fragmented buyers, long sales cycles
- Low tech-forwardness in target market
