# GovProcure AI — Overview

## What It Is
B2B SaaS for US government contractors to find RFPs faster.
AI-matches SAM.gov opportunities to a contractor's NAICS codes, past wins, and agency relationships — 24/7, automatically.

## The Problem
- SAM.gov is the primary government contract database but has terrible UX
- Capture managers spend 3-4 hours/day manually checking for new opportunities
- Missing a pre-solicitation = losing 6-12 months of relationship-building runway
- GovWin IQ (the incumbent) costs $40K/year — unaffordable for most small firms

## ICP
- **Who**: Capture Managers, BD Directors, Proposal Managers at small-to-mid GovCon firms
- **Company**: 10-500 employees, any small business set-aside (8(a), SDVOSB, HUBZone, SBIR)
- **Geography**: DC/MD/VA metro, Huntsville AL, Colorado Springs, San Antonio, San Diego
- **Budget**: Can't justify $40K/year for GovWin; pays $300-1000/mo for BD tools
- **Pain (verbatim)**: "I missed a $2M IDIQ because I didn't see the pre-solicitation"

## Pricing Architecture
| Tier | Price | Margin | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Developer | $299/mo | 94% | Solo BD, small firms |
| Team | $999/mo | 90% | 5-20 person BD teams |
| Enterprise | $2,999/mo + usage | 79% | Large primes, integrators |
| CMMC Add-on | +$299/mo | 92% | Defense contractors needing compliance |
