# ScamAlert — Scam Detection for Seniors

## What It Is
Multi-vector scam detection tool for seniors (60+) and their adult children.
Checks URLs, phone numbers, messages, images, and IPs in one tool.
Delivery: WhatsApp bot + web checker (MVP); mobile app (later).

## ICP
- B2C: Seniors 60+, adult children ($7.99/mo caregiver dashboard)
- B2B Primary: Regional banks ($5-10K/mo, highest immediate leverage)
- B2B Secondary: Senior living facilities ($299/mo, 4-6mo sales cycle)
- B2B Long-term: Insurance companies ($0.50-2/policy/mo)

## Monetization Path to $10K MRR
- Month 4-5: B2C ($2.5-4K) + Banks ($10-15K) + Affiliate ($0.5-2K) = **$12.9-21K total**
- Primary lever: 3 bank pilots at $5K/mo = $15K/mo
