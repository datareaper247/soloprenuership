# Founder Log — Personal Pattern Accrual

> This file is your personal playbook. Auto-generated by SoloOS at the end of sessions containing key decisions, experiments, or pivots. Unlike the static knowledge base (generic founder patterns), this file contains YOUR patterns with YOUR outcomes.
>
> After 6 months, this is worth more than any generic framework — it's calibrated to your specific market, your specific biases, and your specific mistakes and wins.
>
> **Do not delete entries.** Outdated entries become evidence of how your thinking evolved.

---

## HOW TO READ THIS FILE

- `[PENDING OUTCOME]` = outcome not yet known. Claude will ask at next session after due date.
- `✅ CONFIRMED` = hypothesis validated. Pattern is real.
- `❌ INVALIDATED` = hypothesis wrong. Anti-pattern for you specifically.
- `🔄 PARTIAL` = partially right. Nuance documented.

Each entry is linked via `[[FL-XXX]]` and can be referenced from context files, decision logs, and experiments.
