## 1. Alignment decision

The company is not simply:

- a Dutch ZZP tool;
- a SoloOS/soloprenuership project;
- a TapTap/SpatialSense parent;
- a consulting brand;
- a social-media experiment.

The company is a broader **AI-first founder-led software company** using senior engineering expertise in ecommerce/logistics and product execution to build B2B/B2C apps, SaaS/micro-SaaS, consulting, R&D, OSS, content authority, and multiple revenue streams.
