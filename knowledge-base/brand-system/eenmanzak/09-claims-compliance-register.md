# Claims and Compliance Verification Register

**Status:** Draft; source-linked operational baseline; professional review required before publication
**Last updated:** 2026-04-17
**Primary source IDs:** E01-E56, U07
**Scope:** Public claims, founder/company-site copy, social posts, lead capture, support, AI-content, creator partnerships, and platform/account setup for the Eenmanszaak / Eenmanzak brand system.

> This register is a practical claims-control tool. It is not legal, tax, immigration, privacy, accounting, or advertising advice. Use it to keep public wording conservative until a qualified Dutch/EU professional verifies the exact implementation.

## How to use this register

Before publishing a website page, LinkedIn profile update, social post, Product Hunt page, creator brief, testimonial, support reply, or lead form:

1. Find the relevant claim family below.
2. Use the **safe wording** column unless a professional reviewer approves stronger wording.
3. Avoid the prohibited wording.
4. Keep the source ID in the draft or review note.
5. Escalate when the **review gate** says so.

## Copy-ready safe wording library

Use these defaults unless a professional reviewer approves stronger wording:

- “General information only; verify legal, tax, immigration, accounting, privacy, and regulatory decisions with a qualified professional.”
- “The company site is the canonical source for current company identity, proof, support, privacy, and contact details.”
- “We maintain a processing register for recurring forms, newsletter signups, support requests, social DMs copied into business systems, testimonials, and creator/vendor workflows.”
- “Tracking cookies and advertising pixels are only enabled after active consent and clear notice.”
- “Newsletter signups use explicit opt-in and every email includes an easy unsubscribe path.”
- “Testimonials, quotes, logos, and screenshots are published only with documented permission for the approved wording and channels.”
- “AI may assist drafts and repurposing, but a human reviews and approves public content.”
- “Synthetic or meaningfully altered realistic media is labeled/disclosed where platform rules or law require it.”
- “Creator partnerships must be clearly disclosed as sponsored, paid, gifted, affiliate, or otherwise commercial where applicable.”
- “Founder profile wording should keep current senior-engineer credibility visible while linking to the official company site for proof.”

## Absolute “do not publish” list

Do not publish these without direct evidence and professional/platform review:

- “Verified by LinkedIn/GitHub/Product Hunt” unless badge/page status exists.
- “KVK registered,” “VAT registered,” “WBSO approved,” or “KOR applies” unless current documents confirm.
- “Trademarked,” “protected brand,” or “official mark” unless BOIP/EUIPO registration/clearance supports it.
- “GDPR compliant,” “AI Act compliant,” “EAA compliant,” or “fully accessible” without a review/audit basis.
- “No accountant/lawyer needed.”
- “Guaranteed tax savings,” “guaranteed compliance,” “guaranteed results.”
- “Safe navigation,” “medical-grade,” or “replaces assistive devices” for SpatialSense-style products.
- Any customer quote, logo, screenshot, or case result without permission record.
- Any paid/gifted/affiliate creator content without clear disclosure.
