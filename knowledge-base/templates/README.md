## Core Templates (Inline)

### Business Hypothesis
```
FOR [specific customer segment with a job title or clear description]
WHO struggle with [specific, observable problem they're actively experiencing]
WE BUILD [product type: SaaS, API, tool, service]
THAT DELIVERS [core value proposition in one sentence]
UNLIKE [primary alternative they use today]
OURS [key differentiator — measurable or distinctive]
WE BELIEVE [X% of target customers] will pay [$Y/month]
WE'LL KNOW WE'RE RIGHT WHEN [specific measurable signal — e.g., "10 pre-sales at $99/month"]
TIMELINE TO VALIDATE: [X weeks]
```

### Opportunity Scorecard
```markdown
## [Opportunity Name]
**Date**: [Date]
**Analyst**: [Name/AI Agent]

### Scores (1-10)

| Dimension | Score | Evidence |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Problem Severity | /10 | [What evidence proves this?] |
| Market Size (TAM) | /10 | [$Xm TAM, $Xm SAM] |
| Competitive Gap | /10 | [What gap exists?] |
| Willingness to Pay | /10 | [Who's paying for what today?] |
| Solo Buildability | /10 | [Can 1 person + AI build v1 in 4 weeks?] |
| **TOTAL** | **/50** | |

**Weighted Score**: [X]/10

### Decision
- [ ] PURSUE (Score ≥ 6.5)
- [ ] INVESTIGATE FURTHER (Score 5-6.5)
- [ ] PASS (Score < 5)

### Recommended Next Step
[Specific action to take if pursuing]
```

### Customer Interview Script
```markdown
# Customer Interview: [Session #]
**Date**: [Date]
**Interviewee**: [Role/Company — anonymized]
**Duration**: 30 minutes

## Context Setting (2 min)
"Thanks for taking the time. I want to understand how you currently
handle [problem area] — not pitch anything. Can you walk me through
your typical week when it comes to [X]?"

## Problem Exploration (10 min)
1. "What's the most frustrating part of [process]?"
2. "How much time does that take per week?"
3. "Have you tried to solve it? What happened?"
4. "On a scale of 1-10, how important is solving this?"
   [If < 7: probably not the right problem]

## Current Solution Audit (5 min)
5. "What tools do you use for this today?"
6. "What do you love about your current approach?"
7. "What would make you switch to something else?"

## Willingness to Pay Probe (5 min)
8. "If there was a tool that [solved X] automatically, would that be valuable?"
   [If yes:] "What would you expect to pay for that?"
   [Probe: "What if it was $X/month?"]

## Wrap Up (2 min)
9. "Is there anyone else I should talk to who deals with this?"
10. "Can I follow up as we build this?"

## Post-Interview Notes
**Key insight**:
**Surprising finding**:
**Problem confirmed?** Yes/No/Partially
**WTP signal**: $X/month
**Quote to remember**: "[exact quote]"
```
