# E2E Job Skills Library

The complete skill set required to run a billion-dollar company as a solo founder, systematized into AI-executable playbooks.

## Skill Categories

### CEO Skills
- Vision & strategy formulation
- Investor narrative construction
- Board/stakeholder communication
- Hiring (of AI agents and humans)
- Culture & values definition
- Crisis management
- Partnership & deal negotiation

### CTO Skills
- Architecture design (monolith → microservices → serverless)
- Tech stack selection framework
- Security & compliance baseline
- AI/ML integration patterns
- Technical debt management
- Engineering hiring & team structure
- Open source vs build vs buy decisions

### CMO Skills
- Brand positioning & messaging
- Content strategy & editorial calendar
- SEO & organic growth systems
- Paid acquisition frameworks
- Community building
- Launch playbooks (PH, HN, Reddit)
- Email marketing automation

### CFO Skills
- Unit economics modeling (CAC, LTV, payback)
- Revenue recognition
- Fundraising narrative
- Cap table management
- Financial projections
- Tax optimization (solo founder)
- Pricing strategy

### COO Skills
- Process documentation
- Automation architecture
- Vendor management
- Customer success systems
- OKR frameworks
- Weekly operating cadence
- Incident management

### Research Skills
- Primary market research (customer interviews)
- Secondary research (web, databases)
- Competitive intelligence
- Trend analysis & forecasting
- Hypothesis formation & testing
- Data synthesis & insight extraction

### Growth Skills
- Acquisition channel testing
- Activation optimization
- Retention mechanics
- Referral & viral loops
- Pricing experiments
- Expansion revenue

## Usage

Each skill has a corresponding:
- `prompt.md` — Claude/AI system prompt for that role
- `workflow.md` — Step-by-step execution guide
- `templates/` — Output templates
- `examples/` — Real-world examples
