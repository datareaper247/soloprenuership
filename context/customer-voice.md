# Customer Voice
# The most valuable document in your business.
# Real words from real customers. Never paraphrase — exact quotes only.
# Use this for: copy, positioning, cold outreach hooks, product decisions, investor decks.

---

## How to Use This File

1. After every customer call, paste the best quotes here
2. After reading reviews (G2, App Store, Trustpilot), paste the most vivid language
3. When writing any copy, search this file first — customers describe the pain better than you do
4. Claude reads this automatically and uses these words in all outreach and copy tasks

**The #1 copywriting rule**: Use THEIR words, not yours. A customer saying "I was drowning in
spreadsheets" is worth 10x more than you writing "we simplify your workflow."
