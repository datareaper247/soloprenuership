# Experiment Log
# Track every growth experiment. Learn faster than competitors.
# The compound effect: 52 experiments/year = asymmetric learning advantage.

---

## Why Track Experiments

Most founders repeat failed experiments (forgot they tried it) and abandon successful ones
(forgot what made it work). This file prevents both.

**The rule**: If you spend more than 2 hours on a growth initiative, it goes here.

---
