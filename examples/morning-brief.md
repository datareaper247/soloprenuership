**Setup**: `context/business-context.md` populated with Rankly context (keyword rank tracker,
$4,200 MRR, 140 customers, 6 weeks flat growth, 2 open decisions, current OKR: $6K MRR by end of quarter).

## What This Example Demonstrates

1. **Pulse is honest, not cheerleading** — "this quarter is likely a miss" is more useful than optimism
2. **One thing is actually one thing** — P1 is explicit, with the email draft included
3. **"NOT TODAY" list is as valuable as the to-do list** — prevents founder distraction
4. **Decision of the day forces resolution** — not "think about it more" but "decide today, here's why"
5. **Context files make this possible** — without `business-context.md`, this is generic. With it, it's calibrated.

The difference between `/morning` without context and with context:

| Without context | With context |
|----------------|-------------|
| "Set your priorities for today" | "You have 11 days to close $1,800 gap — here's the realistic path" |
| "Follow up on leads" | "Your 2 warmest prospects are 9+ days cold — send this specific email" |
| "Make a decision today" | "Free trial decision has been open 14 days — here's why to decide now" |
